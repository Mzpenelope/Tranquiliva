About Us

We dream of a world where every African can access mental health support without barriers.

Breaking Barriers to Mental Health Support

You deserve care that addresses depression, stress, and the challenges you face every day. At Tranquiliva, we connect you with the right resources, guidance, and community to take charge of your mental well-being.

  • Access resources that address depression and stress
  • Find guidance and support to navigate life’s challenges
  • Join a community committed to normalizing mental health care

We aim to change how mental health is understood and addressed, breaking stigma and providing care to anyone in need.
