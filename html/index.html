    Why Tranquiliva

    We understand the stigma, the silence, and the struggle that often come with mental health challenges. That’s why we’re building a safe space for anyone to find support, understanding, and guidance without judgment.

    We aim to be a go-to platform where mental health care is accessible, quick, and affordable for everyone, no matter their background or location.

    Mental health affects everyone, yet access to care is still a barrier for many. With Tranquiliva, we aim to break the stigma and provide practical, immediate solutions for those in need. By putting help just a few clicks away, we’re empowering people to take control of their mental health journey.

    In an emergency? Need help now?

    If you’re feeling overwhelmed or need urgent support, don’t wait. We’re here to help you navigate through tough times and find the care you need.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers to some of the most common questions about our mental health services and support.

    What types of therapy sessions do you offer?

    We offer individual therapy, couples therapy, family therapy, and children’s therapy. Each session is tailored to meet the unique needs of the client.

    How much do therapy sessions cost?

    Our therapy sessions are affordable, starting at GHS 40 per hour for children and GHS 50 per hour for individual sessions. Family therapy is GHS 120 per hour, and couples therapy is GHS 80 per hour.

    Are there any free resources available?

    Yes! We provide free access to mental health resources, self-help guides, and a supportive community network to help you on your journey.

    Can I book therapy sessions online?

    Yes, you can easily book sessions online through our website. Simply select your preferred therapist and available time slots.

    Do you provide therapy for children?

    Absolutely. Our children’s therapy sessions are designed for kids aged 5–12 years and focus on stress management, emotional development, and behavioral support.

    What qualifications do your therapists have?

    Our therapists are certified professionals with extensive experience in mental health care. They specialize in various fields to provide the best support for clients.

    How do I know which type of therapy is right for me?

    You can complete our online questionnaire, designed to assess your unique needs and challenges. Based on your responses, we will match you with the most suitable therapist and therapy type.
